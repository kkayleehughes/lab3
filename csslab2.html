Minimalism Today

The minimalism art style made way in the 60s and 70s. This started to take over the abstract movement that had just taken over the years prior.

Carl Andre, Dan Flavin, Donald Judd, Sol LeWitt, Agnes Martin and Robert Morris were artists who inspired this movement. The minimalism movement is known as the highest form of pure beauty, the simplicity and cleanliness of the art style bring calmness which draws people in.
